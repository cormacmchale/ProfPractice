Going My Way/RideShare

Welcome to RideShare app

In order to use the app you need to be registered and logged in

As soon as you did that you can either search a journey or offer one

In order to plan a journey click at the "hamburger menu icon" in the top left corner of the app Select the tab: plan a journey. Type in the location you are currently in in the search bar. Press search button. Keep pressing the marker and drag it to your destinantion Press the "Add This Journey" button at the top right Congratulations you've just added a journey :)

In order to view all the journeys go to the side menu Click the tab "Map" Click "Show Journies" button at the top right Markers with all the journeys available will appear in the
